Exhibits and Activities

During a GCM visit, children try on many identities to help explore the world around them. Whether they help a pet patient in the Vet Clinic or leap to stardom in our Twinkle Stars Theater, play is critical to our experience. We offer various different types of activities for each exhibit to encourage as much play as we possibly can. Let's take a look at some of our activities.

Our Mission

We create and foster engaging, impactful PLAY experiences for children and families to learn and thrive. Our vision is a community that values PLAY as the foundation for the learning and development of children and families across generations.
